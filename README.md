Medical Patient Management System

A medical system designed to capture, manage, and review patient information, allowing doctors to maintain a complete and organized medical history for each patient.

The platform stores personal data, medical background, diagnoses, consultations, treatments, and patient progress, providing a centralized and accurate clinical record.
The goal is to improve the quality of care, streamline medical processes, and ensure that medical histories remain accessible, secure, and up to date.

Features

Patient registration and demographic data.

Medical history tracking.

Record of diagnoses, consultations, and treatments.

Centralized patient information.

Secure handling of medical data.

Tracking of patient progress and follow-ups.
